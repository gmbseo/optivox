# Conversion Optimization Strategy

## Primary conversion goal

**Qualified leads** via Free Google Visibility Audit (primary) and Book a Consultation (secondary).

## CTA placement

| Location | CTA |
|----------|-----|
| Header (desktop + mobile) | Free audit button |
| Hero (every major page) | Audit + Consultation |
| Audit lead magnet (home) | Dual CTA + checklist |
| Service/industry page footer | Audit CTA |
| Contact page | Full form |
| Final CTA (home) | Dual CTA |

## Lead magnet: Free Google Visibility Audit

**What is reviewed:**
- Website technical and on-page SEO
- Google Business Profile completeness and optimization
- Local rankings vs. competitors
- Citation and NAP consistency (where applicable)

**What prospect receives:**
- Summary of findings
- Competitive context
- Prioritized action plan

**Timeline:** 3–5 business days

**Value proposition:** Actionable clarity with no obligation—useful even without hiring Optivox.

## Trust indicators

- Client testimonials with names, roles, and businesses
- Case studies with metrics (calls, rankings, leads)
- Results section with aggregate outcome stats
- Transparent process (4-step how-it-works)
- No paid ads positioning (reduces skepticism about upsells)

## Objection handling (FAQ)

- Timeline to results
- No paid ads focus
- Who it's for (SMB local businesses)
- What makes Optivox different
- How the free audit works

## Form optimization

Contact form fields match B2B qualification needs:
- Name, business name, website, email, phone, business goals
- Intent hidden field (audit vs. consultation)
- Success state with clear next-step messaging
- Minimal friction: website optional

## Mobile UX

- Sticky header with menu + CTA
- Single-column layouts on small screens
- Large tap targets on buttons and form fields
- Click-to-call phone link on contact page

## Future enhancements

- Live chat for high-intent visitors
- CRM integration with lead scoring
- A/B testing on hero headlines
- Industry-specific landing page variants per geo
